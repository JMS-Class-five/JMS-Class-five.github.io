Family Education

Here are some manners that children should e advised to use in public.

First, say hello to people they know. They can also shake hands with people if they meet for the first time.
Second, use “indoor voices” indoors. They should keep quiet or keep their voices down in the places like libraries, hospitals. Never shout or laugh loudly in public.
Also, never throw any rubbish on the ground. To keep the environment clean, they shouldn't drop litter everywhere.
And, if they bump into someone, they should always say,"Excuse me." What's more, it's important that they learn to wait for their turn.
